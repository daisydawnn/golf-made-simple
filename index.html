FAQs

How do I schedule my lessons?

Your first lesson will be ready within 48 hours of booking. After each lesson, your next one will be delivered within another 48 hours—often even faster (usually 24).

What equipment do I need to get started?

You’ll need a smartphone to record your swing and ideally your own golf clubs. If you’re just getting started and don’t have clubs yet, we can still help guide your first steps.

How long do I have to use my lessons?

We suggest clients complete one lesson per week, but your lessons do not expire at the moment. Take them at a pace that works best for you.

What if I’m a complete beginner?

Perfect! Many of our clients are just starting out. Our step-by-step video feedback and personalized drills make it easy to build fundamentals at your own pace.

How do I record my swing?

Once you sign up, we’ll send you a quick guide on how to record from the right angles using your phone. You don’t need a fancy setup—we’ll help you get everything you need with just a smartphone.

Can I message my coach with questions?

Yes! You’ll have access to your coach through the mobile app, so you can ask questions, get clarifications, and stay on track between lessons.

How can I reach you for more questions?

You can DM us anytime on Instagram @madesimple.golf — we’re happy to help!
